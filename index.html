Privacy Policy – KHAIPHI MLT

Effective date: 2025-07-28

This Privacy Policy describes how KHAIPHI MLT (developed by Nguyen Van Hoang / Viet Vuong) handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

KHAIPHI MLT does not collect any personal data from users within the app.

Account creation is handled separately by authorized managers via a management website. No data input is required from users in the mobile app directly.

2. Push Notifications

The app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications. FCM may collect device-related data (such as device tokens and IP address) to deliver messages. You can read more about Firebase’s Privacy Policy.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with any third parties.

4. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including secure communications via HTTPS.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
